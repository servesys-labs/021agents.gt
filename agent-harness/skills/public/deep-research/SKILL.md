# Deep Research Methodology

When asked to research a topic, follow this structured methodology:

## Phase 1: Broad Exploration
- Search with multiple keywords and phrasings
- Identify key sources, authors, and terminology
- Map the landscape of the topic

## Phase 2: Deep Dive
- Follow references and primary sources
- Cross-reference findings across multiple sources
- Identify contradictions and areas of consensus

## Phase 3: Synthesis
- Combine findings into a structured output
- Highlight key findings with confidence levels
- Note gaps in available information
- Provide actionable recommendations

Use this skill BEFORE generating content that requires factual accuracy.
